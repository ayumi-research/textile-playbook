## Archive: Design Decisions

### Why 9 Lessons?

**Z's preference:** "The longer, the better." Comprehensive > Concise.

**Alternative considered:** 7 lessons (merge 6+7, 8+9) — rejected.

### The Arc Flow

```
Foundation (1-3) → Vetting (4) → Cost Reality (5) → Execution (6-7) → Recovery (8) → Reference (9)
```

**Logic:** Each lesson builds on previous. Can't vet suppliers (4) without understanding techniques (1). Can't manage cost (5) without knowing quotes (2). Can't handle problems (8) without prototypes (7).
