Pricing Benchmarks That Actually Exist

Quick summary
Real numbers from the German / EU textile market — what embroidery, screen print, and DTF actually cost per piece, what setup fees look like, and where suppliers make their margin.
Why it saves
Walk into a supplier negotiation knowing what “fair” looks like. Spot the padded quote before you commit.
Bottom line
Walk into negotiations with ranges, not asks. “I’m seeing €X–€Y” beats “Can you do €X?”

The “All-In” Ask

Never negotiate on sticker price. Negotiate on delivered cost. “I need 150 items, chest logo, delivered to [city] by [date]. What's the all-in price including digitizing, samples, shipping, and any rush fees?” Then compare apples to apples: same product tier, same decoration complexity, same timeline, same inclusions.
