Privacy policy.

How we handle the small amount of data that touches this site. Written in plain English; the legal bases (Art. 6 GDPR) are cited where relevant.

Last updated: May 2026 · Get Map UG (haftungsbeschränkt)
◆ The short version

This site uses Google Tag Manager to coordinate tracking scripts. Tracking is consent-based — a cookie banner lets you accept or reject cookies before they fire. Fonts are self-hosted. If you buy the playbook, the payment is handled by Paddle (acting as merchant of record) — they collect the minimum data needed to process the order and issue a VAT invoice.

02 What we collect on this site

This site uses Google Tag Manager (GTM) to coordinate tracking scripts. GTM loads a small container from googletagmanager.com (Google LLC). GTM itself sets a temporary technical cookie to coordinate tag firing.

Tracking is consent-based: a cookie banner is shown on first visit. Until you accept, tracking scripts remain inactive. You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the Cookie Settings button in the footer.

The only other browser storage used is a small localStorage entry that remembers which page of the playbook you were last reading, so the book reopens where you left it. This value never leaves your browser.

03 Server logs (hosting)

The site is served from a virtual private server running Nginx. When your browser requests a page, the web server automatically records technical access data — your IP address, the requested URL, the date and time, the response status, the referring URL, and your browser’s user agent string. These logs are automatically deleted after 30 days. They are not combined with any other data source and are not used to identify individual visitors.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR — legitimate interest in a stable, secure website.

Fonts (Space Grotesk, JetBrains Mono) are self-hosted and served from this domain.

04 Email contact

If you email us, your message and email address are stored for the purpose of handling your inquiry and any follow-up. These messages are not shared with third parties. Data is deleted once the matter is resolved, and at the latest after 6 months of inactivity, unless statutory retention applies (§147 AO requires up to 10 years for tax-relevant correspondence).

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR — pre-contractual or contractual communication — or Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR for general inquiries.

05 Payment processing (Paddle)

Purchases of the playbook are processed by Paddle.com Market Ltd., Judd House, 18–29 Mora Street, London EC1V 8BT, United Kingdom. Paddle acts as the merchant of record: when you buy, your contract of sale is with Paddle, and Paddle resells the product to you. Paddle collects the billing data necessary to complete the transaction — name, email, billing address, country, VAT status, and payment-method details — and is responsible for handling EU VAT on the sale.

We receive only the minimum information we need to fulfil your order: your email address (to send the access link), the country of sale, the order ID, and the amount. We do not see or store your card, bank, or PayPal details at any time.

Paddle’s own privacy policy applies to the data it collects directly: paddle.com/legal/privacy.

Order data we receive from Paddle is retained for up to 10 years under §257 HGB / §147 AO (commercial and tax retention).

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (b) GDPR — performance of the sales contract.

06 External resources loaded by your browser

When you view this site, your browser fetches a few resources from third-party CDNs. These providers may receive your IP address and basic request metadata (referrer, user agent) simply because your browser connects to their servers:

  • unpkg (Cloudflare) — the React and Babel libraries that power the interactive reader are loaded from unpkg.com, a CDN operated by Cloudflare. Cloudflare’s privacy policy: cloudflare.com/privacypolicy. Cloudflare Inc. is certified under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (f) GDPR — legitimate interest in serving a fast, working site via established CDNs.

06a Google Tag Manager

We use Google Tag Manager (container ID GTM-NK8VJHZS) to manage tracking scripts on this site. GTM loads from googletagmanager.com (Google LLC, 1600 Amphitheatre Parkway, Mountain View, CA 94043, USA).

GTM itself does not perform analytics — it only coordinates which tracking tags are active based on your cookie consent. The actual data collected depends on the tags loaded (currently: Matomo analytics). All tracking is consent-based per § 25 TDDDG.

You can change or withdraw your consent at any time via the Cookie Settings link in the site footer. Withdrawing consent stops future tag firings and, for Matomo, applies retroactively.

Google LLC processes data under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework (EU Commission adequacy decision of July 10, 2023). Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) also apply.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent) + § 25 TDDDG.

06b Web analytics (Matomo)

We use Matomo (open-source web analytics) to understand how visitors use this site. Matomo is self-hosted on our own server in Germany at matomo.textileplaybook.com — data does not leave our infrastructure unless explicitly exported.

Matomo collects the following data about each visit: IP address (anonymized to the last octet), pages viewed, referrer URL, browser and operating system, screen resolution, and time spent on each page. No personal names or email addresses are linked to analytics data unless you explicitly complete a form.

Matomo sets the following cookies:

  • _pk_id — unique visitor identifier; lifetime: 13 months
  • _pk_ses — current session; lifetime: 30 minutes
  • _pk_ref — referrer information; lifetime: session

Analytics data is retained for 24 months by default. You can opt out of Matomo tracking at any time via the Cookie Settings button in the footer, or by visiting the Matomo opt-out page.

Legal basis: Art. 6 (1) (a) GDPR (consent) + § 25 TDDDG.

06c Data security (Art. 32 GDPR)

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • All pages are served over TLS 1.3 (Let’s Encrypt certificates).
  • The server runs behind Nginx with loopback-only services and SSH key authentication.
  • Processor data protection agreements (Art. 28 GDPR) are in place with Paddle, Google LLC, and Cloudflare.

06d Data protection officer

Under § 38 BDSG, Get Map UG is not currently required to appoint a Data Protection Officer (fewer than 20 persons are continuously engaged in the automated processing of personal data). Should this change, we will update this policy. You can address data protection inquiries to hello@textileplaybook.com.

06e Children’s data

This site is not directed at children under the age of 16. If data from a child under 16 is collected (e.g., via email inquiry), processing is based on parental consent or the child’s implied consent, depending on the circumstances.

06f Source of personal data

When you purchase the playbook, we receive your email address, country of sale, order ID, and amount from Paddle (acting as merchant of record). From our perspective as a data controller, this data is received from Paddle under Art. 14 GDPR. Your email address is used to deliver the access link and for order-related communication.

07 Your rights under the GDPR

You have the right to:

  • access the personal data we hold about you (Art. 15 GDPR)
  • request correction of inaccurate data (Art. 16 GDPR)
  • request deletion (Art. 17 GDPR) and restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • receive your data in a portable format (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21 GDPR)
  • lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority (Art. 77 GDPR) — for us, the Bavarian Data Protection Authority (BayLDA, Ansbach)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@textileplaybook.com. For payment-related data held by Paddle, please contact Paddle directly using the link in section 05.

08 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if we change the site, add a new processor, or if the law changes. The “last updated” date above always reflects the current version. Substantive changes affecting existing customers will be notified by email.
